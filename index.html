About the League

The Ottawa-Gatineau Hockey Association (OGHA) is a not-for-profit organization representing hockey in the Ottawa-Gatineau metropolitan area. The association offers a friendly sporting environment to all persons with an interest in ice hockey. The OGHA offers competitive and recreational levels of play and is committed to fostering and developing its members' passion for hockey. There are 4 divisions in a winter and summer league and we have ice times at 4 different surfaces around the city.
